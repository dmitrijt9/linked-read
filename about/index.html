Best way to use linked data

Linked read uses benefits of a vast world of semantic web. It is trying to make your love to reading even more interesting while using idea of open and linked data.

Features

In Linked Read you can easily search books and authors and then see found results in an interactive network map. Where nodes are books, authors or series and links between nodes are relations such as - "Author written a book." or "Book belongs to a series."

Search books
Find books by just typing a part of a title of the book you are looking for.
Search authors
Find authors by just typing a name or a surname of the author you are looking for.
Discover relationships
See what books an author written or what series he created. Or look to what series a book belongs to. All this in an interactive network map, so you see all in one place.
Book detail
Select a node representing a book and look for some details about it.
Set up a map
Set up a network map as you wish. You'd like to group nodes tight, bigger nodes or larger font size? No problem.
